Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Why I Admire Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was not just a brilliant scientist, but also a humble human being and an inspiring leader. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work in ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he later served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007).

Despite his achievements, he remained grounded and connected to students and youth across the country. His speeches and books, especially *Wings of Fire*, have left a lasting impact on generations of Indians.

Admirable Qualities:

  • Visionary Thinker
  • Humble and Kind
  • Dedicated to Education
  • Passionate Scientist
  • Inspiring Leader
